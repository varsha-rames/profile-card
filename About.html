When did tacos first appear in the united state?

Originally, the taco appeared in the U.S. from migrants moving from Mexico to Los Angeles in the early 1900s. At the time, it was seen as low-class street food due to how inexpensive they were. When the taco came to America, it was difficult to find the authentic ingredients used in Mexico.
